How to Setup Mozilla Thunderbird

Quick steps:

  1. Download and install Thunderbird

    Visit https://www.thunderbird.net, click Download, and follow the prompts.

  2. Open Thunderbird

    Open the installer and complete the setup. Then click the â˜° menu in the top right corner.

  3. Set up your email account

    Click on New Account.

  4. Select Email

    Choose Email.

  5. Enter your name, email address, and password

    Thunderbird may auto-detect server settings. If not, enter them manually.

  6. Enter the server names and ports

    Fill out the required server details provided by your email provider.

  7. Click Done

    Finish setup by clicking Done.
