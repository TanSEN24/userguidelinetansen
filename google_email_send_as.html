How to Setup Email Send As

In this guide, we will show you how to set up your email account to send emails as another address. This is useful if you want to manage multiple email addresses from a single account.

Quick steps:

  1. Go to Google Chrome and search "Create new Gmail account".

    Click on Create a Gmail account.

  2. If you don't have an account:

    Click on Create account.

  3. Create Google Account

    Enter your first and last name, then click Next.

  4. Enter Your Date of Birth and Gender

    Fill in your date of birth and gender, then click Next.

  5. Choose Your Gmail Address

    Select or create a Gmail address, then click Next.

  6. Choose a Password

    Create a strong password and then click Next.

  7. Enter Your Phone Number

    Input your phone number, verify you're not a robot, and then click Next.

  8. Enter the Verification Code

    Enter the code sent to your phone number and click Next.

  9. Add Recovery Email (Optional)

    You can add a recovery email or skip this step.

  10. Review Account Information

    You will see a summary of your info. Click Next.

  11. All Done

    Click on I agree. Your email setup is complete. You can now use your email for school, work, or personal use.
